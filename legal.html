Legal Case Predictor App

The Legal Case Predictor App is a Flask-based web application designed to assist legal professionals in analyzing and classifying legal cases. By leveraging machine learning techniques, the app predicts whether a case pertains to housing or eviction based on the textual information provided. Additionally, it offers visual insights into the most influential words that contribute to the model's predictions.

Key Highlights:

  • Flask Web Application: Provides a user-friendly interface for uploading legal case documents and viewing classification results.
  • Machine Learning Integration: Utilizes trained models to classify cases into categories such as housing or eviction.
  • Explainable AI: Visualizes the most influential words from the models, enhancing transparency and interpretability of predictions.
  • Parameter Configuration: Employs a params.yaml file to manage model parameters and settings efficiently.
  • Sample Input: Includes a test-case.txt file to demonstrate the application's functionality.
